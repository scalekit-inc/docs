# What's in Scalekit?

<Subtitle>

Scalekit equips your B2B SaaS platform with essential enterprise features like Single Sign-On and SCIM provisioning. Designed to integrate seamlessly with all major identity providers so that you don't have to

</Subtitle>

What your can achieve with Scalekit:

- **Seamless Single Sign-On (SSO) Integration**: Effortlessly connect with major identity providers your customers already use.
- **Automated User Provisioning**: Enable automatic access provisioning for enterprise users based on directory memberships, supporting both SCIM and non-SCIM protocols.
- **Simplified Social Logins**: Let Scalekit handle social authentication providers like Google and Microsoft, ensuring a smooth login experience.
- **Customer Admin Portal**: Empower your customers with a self-service portal to configure their identity provider integrations.
